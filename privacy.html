Privacy Policy

隐私政策 / プライバシーポリシー

Effective Date: March 2026 / 生效日期：2026年3月

Summary: DailyNav respects your privacy. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

简要说明：DailyNav 尊重您的隐私。您的数据存储在本地设备上，我们不会收集、传输或出售您的个人信息。

1. Data Collection / 数据收集

DailyNav stores all user data (goals, tasks, journal entries, mood records, achievements) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. We do not collect any personal data, usage analytics, or behavioral information.

DailyNav 将所有用户数据（目标、任务、心得日记、心情记录、成就）存储在您设备的本地存储中。我们不会收集任何个人数据、使用分析或行为信息。

2. AI Features (Pro) / AI 功能

Pro subscribers may use AI-powered summary features. When you use these features, your journal and goal data may be processed by a third-party AI service to generate summaries. This data is:

  • Transmitted securely via HTTPS
  • Not stored by the AI provider after processing
  • Not used for training AI models

Pro 订阅用户可能使用 AI 智能总结功能。使用这些功能时，您的日记和目标数据可能会被第三方 AI 服务处理以生成总结。这些数据通过 HTTPS 安全传输，处理后不会被 AI 提供商保留，也不会用于训练 AI 模型。

3. In-App Purchases / 应用内购买

Subscriptions and purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. For subscription management, please visit iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

订阅和购买完全通过 Apple App Store 处理，我们无法获取您的支付信息。管理订阅请前往 iOS 设置 > Apple ID > 订阅。

4. iCloud Sync / iCloud 同步

If you enable iCloud sync (Pro feature), your data will be stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is managed by Apple's iCloud service and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

如果您启用了 iCloud 同步（Pro 功能），您的数据将存储在您的个人 iCloud 账户中，由 Apple 的 iCloud 服务管理。

5. Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

DailyNav does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

DailyNav 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪 SDK。

6. Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

DailyNav is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

DailyNav 适合所有年龄段（分级 4+），我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的数据。

7. Changes / 变更

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

我们可能会更新此政策。更新将发布在此页面并标注新的生效日期。

8. Contact / 联系方式

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@dailynav.app

如有关于隐私政策的问题，请联系：support@dailynav.app
